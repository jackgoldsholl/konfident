The problem you're living with

Two numbers quietly define the economics of running a DTC store, and they pull in opposite directions from the same root cause: shoppers who can't quickly decide whether a product is right for them.

On the front end, they hesitate. The Baymard Institute puts average documented cart abandonment at ~70%—the majority of carts never make it to a completed order. On the back end, the ones who do buy return at a rate that eats straight into margin. According to the National Retail Federation and Appriss Retail, Americans returned $743 billion of merchandise in 2023, and the online return rate was 17.6%—nearly double the roughly 10% rate for pure brick-and-mortar stores.

17.6%
The 2023 online return rate—almost double the ~10% brick-and-mortar rate. Selling online means absorbing a structurally higher rate of returns, most of which start as a pre-purchase confidence gap.
Source: NRF & Appriss Retail, 2023

Both problems trace back to the same moment on the product page. A shopper is trying to answer one narrow, personal question—"will this actually work for me?"—and a star average plus a wall of individual reviews doesn't answer it. Some shoppers give up and abandon. Others buy on a hopeful guess, and when the guess is wrong, it comes back as an expectation-gap return.

What Konfident does for your store

Konfident reads the reviews you already have and synthesizes them per SKU into a confidence signal on the product page—so the decision is easier and better-matched before checkout.

📈

Higher-confidence buyers

Shoppers already rely on reviews—Baymard found 95% of users leaned on them, and Yotpo reports review-engaged shoppers are ~161% more likely to convert. Konfident does the reading for them, so the confidence they need to buy is on the page instead of buried in it.

↩️

Fewer expectation-gap returns

Most returns are mismatches decided before checkout—wrong fit, different in person, not suited to the use case. By setting expectations honestly up front, Konfident helps the right shopper recognize the right product, shrinking the slice of returns that were really just hopeful guesses.

🔧

No rip-and-replace

Keep the review app you already run—Yotpo, Judge.me, Okendo, Stamped, Loox, or another. Konfident reads your reviews where they live and layers a confidence signal on top. There's nothing to migrate and no vendor to switch.

🎚️

A signal you control

Choose positive-only, balanced, or full-spectrum to match your brand voice and category. And when a product has too little review signal to synthesize honestly, the widget stays hidden rather than faking confidence.

Returns don't have to cost you the customer

Handled well, a return isn't the end of the relationship—it's a chance to keep it. Narvar's State of Returns research finds that 96% of shoppers would buy again from a retailer after an "easy" or "very easy" return. That tells you the shopper didn't reject your brand; they rejected the specific match between this shopper and this product.

Konfident works on the front of that problem: matching the right shopper to the right product before they buy, so fewer of them hit the returns flow in the first place. The returns that do happen aren't fatal to loyalty—but the ones you prevent up front never cost you the margin at all.
