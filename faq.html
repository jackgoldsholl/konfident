How it works
What exactly is a "confidence signal"? +
A confidence signal is a short, AI-synthesized statement that tells a shopper — at the moment they're deciding whether to buy — what your existing reviews say about the specific thing they're likely wondering. For a face serum, that might be: "Works well for combination and dry skin. Takes 2–4 weeks to see results. 91% of reviewers would buy again." It's not a summary paragraph. It's not a star rating. It's the specific, actionable signal that resolves purchase uncertainty — surfaced automatically from your existing reviews.
Does Konfident replace my review platform? +
No — and that's intentional. Konfident works on top of whatever you already use: Yotpo, Judge.me, Okendo, Stamped, Loox, REVIEWS.io, or any other review platform. It reads your existing reviews and synthesizes them into a confidence signal. Your reviews stay where they are. Konfident adds a layer of interpretation on top. Think of it as a translation layer between "here are 847 reviews" and "here is what those reviews mean for this shopper."
How does Konfident access my reviews? +
Through a read-only OAuth connection to your review platform. We never modify your reviews, add to them, or write back to your review platform. We read your existing reviews, synthesize the confidence signal, cache it per SKU, and serve it on your product pages. We request only the minimum permissions needed and you can revoke access at any time.
How long does setup take? +
Under 10 minutes for Shopify merchants — it's a single-click install. You connect your review platform via OAuth, and confidence signals start generating immediately for products with enough review volume. No developer, no custom configuration, no onboarding call required. You're live immediately after connecting.
What happens if a product doesn't have many reviews? +
The Konfident widget activates only once a product has enough review signal to synthesize something meaningful. For products with fewer than a threshold of reviews (which varies by review quality and topic coverage), the widget stays hidden rather than showing a thin or potentially misleading summary. As your review catalog grows, more products activate automatically. You don't manage this manually — it happens on its own.
Signal controls
Can I control which reviews power the confidence signal? +
Yes — you have full control over your signal mode:

Positive-only: Surfaces only your strongest, most confident reviews. Maximizes purchase intent, best for products with consistently strong reviews.

Balanced (recommended for most): Includes positive reviews alongside critical context — e.g., "great for oily skin, less ideal for dry" — which tends to build more trust with skeptical shoppers without hiding legitimate concerns.

Full-spectrum: Draws from all reviews including negative and mixed ones. Useful when transparency is a core part of your brand identity. Research from BrightLocal (2026) found that 82% of consumers now read AI-generated review summaries — full-spectrum mode serves this audience well.

You can set a default mode store-wide and override per product. Growth and Pro plans include all three modes; Starter includes positive-only.
Can I customize the widget's appearance? +
The widget inherits your store's theme fonts and brand colors by default, so it looks native to your product page without any configuration. Full custom styling (custom CSS, positioning, layout overrides) is available on the Pro plan. On Starter and Growth, the widget uses a clean, minimal design that works well across most Shopify themes.
Integrations & platforms
Which review platforms does Konfident support? +
Currently supported: Yotpo, Judge.me, Okendo, Stamped.io, Loox, REVIEWS.io, and Bazaarvoice. We're adding more review platforms regularly — contact us if yours isn't listed and we'll tell you when it's coming.
Does Konfident work on WooCommerce or BigCommerce? +
Not yet. We're currently Shopify-only. WooCommerce and BigCommerce support is on the roadmap for Pro plan merchants — if this is blocking for you, let us know and we'll prioritize based on demand.
Does it work with Klaviyo? +
Yes, on the Pro plan. The Klaviyo integration passes confidence signal events to your Klaviyo account — you can use this to trigger post-purchase flows based on whether a shopper engaged with the confidence widget before buying, segment for retention campaigns, or feed return-rate data back into your email strategy. The integration is read/write to Klaviyo event data only.
Pricing & plans
Is there really a free plan? +
Yes. The Starter plan is free for up to 500 monthly orders with no credit card required. It includes the core confidence widget with positive-only signal mode and connects to your existing review platform. It's a real free tier, not a gated demo. See full plan details.
What does "purchase outcome intelligence" mean on the Pro plan? +
Purchase outcome intelligence is a return prediction layer that connects your post-purchase return data to the confidence signals shown at purchase. Over time, it identifies which signals correlate with higher return rates for specific SKUs — letting Konfident refine the synthesis to weight toward review content that correlates with satisfied buyers. It's a data feedback loop that improves signal accuracy. This is optional, and your data is never shared with other merchants.
Privacy & security
Is my data shared with other merchants? +
No. Individual merchant data — including your reviews, SKU data, return rates, and customer information — is never shared with other merchants. If you opt into the purchase outcome intelligence layer (Pro plan), your return data contributes to model improvement in anonymized, aggregated form only. The terms governing this are in our Privacy Policy.
Will Konfident slow down my product pages? +
No. The confidence signal is pre-synthesized and cached per SKU — it loads asynchronously and doesn't block your page rendering pipeline. The widget JavaScript is lightweight by design. We built it specifically to have no measurable impact on page speed scores or Core Web Vitals, because page speed is a conversion factor in its own right and we won't win by hurting it.
What data does Konfident store? +
We store the synthesized confidence signals per SKU (not the raw reviews — those stay in your review platform), widget impression and engagement events (anonymous), and account configuration. We do not store individual shopper PII. Full details are in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
