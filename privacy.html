Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025 · Effective date: June 29, 2025

The short version: We collect only what we need to run Konfident. We don't sell your data. We don't share merchant-specific data with other merchants. Your reviews stay on your review platform — we only read them to synthesize confidence signals. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. Who we are

Konfident, Inc. ("Konfident," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Konfident platform, which provides AI-powered purchase confidence signals for Shopify merchants. We are based in the United States.

For questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@konfident.io.

2. What data we collect

Merchant account data

When you create a Konfident account or request access, we collect: your name, email address, business name, Shopify store URL, and payment information (processed by our payment provider — we don't store card numbers directly).

Review platform data

With your authorization, Konfident connects to your review platform (Yotpo, Judge.me, Okendo, etc.) via read-only OAuth. We read your existing product reviews to synthesize confidence signals. We do not modify, delete, add to, or write back to your reviews. We do not store your raw review text beyond what is necessary to generate and cache the synthesized signals.

Usage and analytics data

We collect data about how the Konfident widget performs on your product pages: widget impressions, engagement events (when a shopper interacts with the widget), and conversion events (when a shopper adds to cart or purchases after viewing the widget). This data is anonymized at the shopper level — we do not collect individual shopper PII through the widget.

Return data (Pro plan, optional)

On the Pro plan, you may optionally connect return data from Shopify. This enables the purchase outcome intelligence feature. This data is used solely to improve the accuracy of confidence signals for your store. It is never shared with other merchants and is anonymized if used in aggregate model improvement.

3. How we use your data

  • To operate the Konfident platform and serve confidence signals on your product pages
  • To synthesize and cache per-SKU confidence signals from your reviews
  • To provide analytics on widget performance in your dashboard
  • To send transactional emails (account setup, billing, service notifications)
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI synthesis models (using anonymized, aggregated data only — never individual merchant data)
  • To respond to support requests and billing inquiries

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, profiling unrelated to service provision, or sale to third parties.

4. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use third-party services to operate Konfident (hosting, payment processing, email delivery). These providers process data only as necessary to provide those services and are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or valid legal process.
  • Business transfer: If Konfident is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you in advance.

We never share merchant-specific data (your reviews, SKUs, return rates, or store analytics) with other merchants or third-party advertisers.

5. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Synthesized confidence signals are cached per SKU and refreshed as reviews are updated. If you cancel your account, we delete your account data within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data (with no merchant-specific identifiers) may be retained for service improvement purposes.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel, and regular security reviews. We use read-only OAuth connections to review platforms and do not store credentials beyond what is necessary for the OAuth flow.

No transmission method over the internet is 100% secure. If you become aware of a security issue involving your Konfident account, please contact us immediately at security@konfident.io.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@konfident.io. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and tracking

The Konfident marketing website (konfident.io) uses essential cookies necessary for site functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell behavioral data to advertising networks.

The Konfident widget installed on your Shopify store uses a minimal session token to track widget engagement anonymously. It does not set persistent third-party cookies or fingerprint individual shoppers.

9. Shopper-facing data

The Konfident widget appears on your merchant's Shopify store product pages. We collect anonymized engagement events (widget viewed, widget expanded, widget interaction before cart add) but do not collect individual shopper PII such as email addresses, names, or device identifiers. Shoppers visiting your store are not required to provide any data to Konfident to view the confidence signal.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email to the account holder and posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of Konfident after a material change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. How to contact us

For privacy questions, data requests, or security concerns:

  • Email: privacy@konfident.io
  • General contact: konfident.io/contact
