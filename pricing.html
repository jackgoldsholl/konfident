Pricing FAQ

What counts as an "order"? +
An order is any completed purchase on your store. We count the total monthly orders processed through your Shopify store, not just orders where the Konfident widget was shown. This makes tracking simple and predictable.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time? +
Yes. You can switch plans at any point from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, so you keep your current features until then. No penalties, no long-term commitments.
What happens if I exceed my monthly order limit? +
We'll notify you when you approach your limit. If you go over, the widget continues to show — we won't disrupt your shoppers mid-month. We'll suggest upgrading at your next billing cycle. We won't charge overage fees without warning.
Is there a free trial on paid plans? +
During the current private beta, we're offering extended trial access on Growth and Pro. Request early access and we'll discuss what makes sense for your store's size and category.
Do you offer discounts for early-stage or small brands? +
Yes. We're in a private beta with a small cohort of early merchants, and pricing is flexible for brands that are a strong fit for the product. If you're a small DTC brand with meaningful review volume, get in touch — we'd rather have you use it than not.
Is my review data secure? +
Your review data is read-only — Konfident never modifies your reviews or adds to them. We access your existing review platform through OAuth or read-only API keys. Individual merchant data is never shared with other merchants. We store only what's needed to generate and cache confidence signals per SKU.
