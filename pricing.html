The details, plainly

What actually differs between plans, and how billing works.

🆚

Free vs. Growth, in practice

Free covers up to 10 SKUs on Shopify with a basic dashboard — enough to see Konfident work on your bestsellers before you commit. Growth lifts you to 100 SKUs, adds WooCommerce and BigCommerce, connects every review-platform integration, and unlocks full analytics plus priority support. If Konfident is earning its keep on your top products, Growth is how you run it across the catalog.

💳

No card to start

The Free plan needs no credit card — install it, connect your reviews, and watch. When you move to a paid plan, early access members get their first 30 days free, and you only enter billing details when that window ends.

🔓

Cancel anytime

No contracts and no setup fees. Paid plans are month-to-month, so you can downgrade or cancel whenever you like. Cancel and the widget simply stops synthesizing — your reviews and your review platform stay exactly where they are.

📈

Upgrade when it pays off

You're never locked into a tier. Start on Free, move to Growth when you want full-catalog coverage, and step up to Pro when you're ready to connect return data for purchase-outcome intelligence. Changes take effect right away and prorate cleanly.
