How it works

Your reviews already
contain the signal.
We surface it.

Konfident connects to your existing review platform, synthesizes what your reviews say per SKU, and delivers a confidence signal directly on the product page — without replacing anything you already use.

The process

Four steps from install to live.

You don't configure the synthesis. You don't write the signals. You connect, and Konfident does the work.

Step 01

Connect your review platform

One OAuth connection links Konfident to your existing review platform. Your reviews stay where they are — we only read them.

Step 02

AI synthesizes reviews per SKU

Konfident reads all reviews for each product variant and identifies the specific patterns shoppers care about — fit, results timeline, use cases, limitations. Then it builds a confidence signal per SKU automatically.

Per-variant synthesis, not per-product
Identifies what review content matters for each category

Widget stays hidden until enough signal exists to be meaningful

How we're different

Review platform vs. Konfident.

We're not a review platform. We're the layer between your reviews and a purchase decision.

Review platforms (Yotpo, Judge.me, etc.)
📥Collect and display reviews
⭐Show star ratings and review counts
🔍Require shoppers to read and interpret reviews themselves
📄Surface raw review content — shopper does the work
❓No per-SKU synthesis or confidence output
Konfident (on top of your review platform)
🧠Synthesizes what reviews say — doesn't collect them
✓Delivers a per-SKU confidence signal, not a rating
⚡Interprets review content so shoppers don't have to
🎯Surfaces the specific signal relevant to each product category
📊Tracks how confidence signals correlate with conversions and returns
