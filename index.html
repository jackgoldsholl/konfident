FAQ

Common questions.

Can I control which reviews power the confidence signal? +
Yes — you have full control over your signal mode. Positive-only surfaces only your strongest, most confident reviews, maximizing purchase intent. Balanced includes positive reviews alongside critical context (e.g. "great for oily skin, less ideal for dry"), which tends to build more trust with skeptical shoppers. Full-spectrum draws from all reviews including negative and mixed ones — useful when transparency is part of your brand. Most merchants start with balanced mode; you can change this per product or store-wide at any time from your dashboard.
Does Konfident replace my review platform? +
No — and that's intentional. Konfident works on top of whatever you already use: Yotpo, Judge.me, Okendo, Stamped, Loox, or any other review platform. It reads your existing reviews and synthesizes them into a confidence signal. Your reviews stay where they are. Konfident adds a layer of interpretation on top.
How long does setup take? +
Under 10 minutes for Shopify merchants — it's a single-click install from the App Store. WooCommerce and BigCommerce merchants add one script tag. No developer, no custom configuration, no onboarding call required. You're live immediately after connecting your review platform.
What if I don't have many reviews yet? +
The Konfident widget activates once a product has enough review signal to synthesize something meaningful. For products with fewer reviews, the widget stays hidden rather than showing a thin summary. As your review catalog grows, more products activate automatically. You don't need to manage this — it happens on its own.
How is this different from just showing reviews? +
Reviews require shoppers to do interpretive work: read through dozens of comments, figure out which ones apply to them, and draw their own conclusions. Most shoppers either spend 13 minutes doing this (and still aren't sure) or abandon the page entirely. Konfident does that interpretive work for them — identifying the specific signal that matters for each product and surfacing it directly, before the shopper has to ask.
Is my return data shared with other merchants? +
No. Individual merchant data is never shared. If you opt into the purchase outcome intelligence layer (Pro plan), your return data is anonymized and aggregated — only broad patterns contribute to model improvement. No other merchant can see your specific return rates, SKU data, or customer information.
Will Konfident slow down my product pages? +
No. The confidence signal is pre-synthesized and cached per SKU — it loads asynchronously and doesn't block your page rendering. The widget is lightweight by design. We've built it specifically to avoid any impact on page speed scores or Core Web Vitals.
