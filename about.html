About

We're building the confidence layer for online shopping.

Konfident is an early-stage, founder-led company. We read the reviews a Shopify store already has and turn them into a clear purchase-confidence signal on the product page — so shoppers can quickly answer the only question that matters: "will this work for me?"

Why we started

Shoppers have more reviews than ever, and are somehow no more certain.

Reviews won. Nearly every product page now has them, and shoppers rely on them heavily — in Baymard Institute's large-scale product-page UX testing, 95% of users relied on reviews, and the ratings-distribution summary was the most-used part of the reviews interface, used even more than the review text itself. Yet the experience of actually using reviews hasn't kept up. You land on a product, see a 4.6 with 800 ratings, and still have to scroll, sort, and read a dozen conflicting opinions to figure out whether it fits your body, your use case, your situation.

When that question goes unanswered, one of two things happens. The shopper abandons — or they guess, buy the wrong thing, and send it back. Returns are the expensive end of that uncertainty: the NRF and Appriss Retail reported an online return rate of 17.6% in 2023, well above the roughly 10% rate for brick-and-mortar. Every one of those returns started as a purchase someone wasn't actually confident about.

That gap — between "there are plenty of reviews" and "I know this is right for me" — is where Konfident started. Not another place to collect reviews, but a layer that interprets the ones a merchant already has.

What we believe

A few honest principles that shape every decision we make.

🔎

Reviews should be interpreted, not just displayed

A wall of star ratings is raw material, not an answer. Our job is to synthesize what the reviews actually say per product and surface it in a sentence, so shoppers don't have to do the reading.

🤝

Confidence beats persuasion

We're not trying to talk anyone into a purchase. We're trying to help the right shopper feel sure and the wrong shopper move on. A confident buyer is a happier customer.

📦

Honesty reduces returns

Overselling wins the checkout and loses the return. That's why our signal has a full-spectrum mode and stays hidden when there's too little review data to say something true.

🔐

The merchant owns their data

We read a store's existing public reviews and never replace its review platform. No shopper PII is collected or stored, return data is anonymized before aggregation, and it's GDPR compliant by design and CCPA ready.

Where we are now

We'd rather tell you exactly what stage we're at than pretend to be bigger than we are.

Konfident is pre-launch and in private beta. We're small, founder-led, and bootstrapped — building deliberately alongside our first merchants rather than chasing a splashy launch. There's no team roster to show off yet, and that's by design: right now the priority is getting the signal right on real stores.

We're onboarding our first merchants through early access. There's no public Shopify App Store listing live yet — it's coming to the Shopify App Store — so the way in today is by request. Early access members help shape the product and get their first 30 days free.
